Empowering Governance Through Expertise and Technology.

Danne Corporate Services is a dynamic firm providing professional corporate governance, compliance, and company secretarial support to businesses across Nigeria. We specialize in helping organizations navigate the complex regulatory landscape with confidence, ensuring that every structure, document, and filing aligns perfectly with the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) and other applicable laws.

With a team of highly skilled governance professionals, Danne Corporate Services bridges the gap between legal compliance and operational efficiency. We take pride in being more than just service providers, we are trusted partners in building sustainable, compliant, and well-governed businesses.

Our firm offers a wide range of corporate support solutions, including:

  • Company Incorporation & Business Registration with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC)
  • Annual Returns Filing & Post-Incorporation Changes
  • Corporate Governance Advisory for boards and management teams
  • Regulatory Compliance Management
  • SMEDAN and Intellectual Property Registration
  • Secretarial & Record-Keeping Services for private and public companies

Through innovation and technology, we are redefining how compliance support is delivered. Our Governance Advisor Bot offers instant guidance to clients simplifying governance procedures and saving valuable time.

At Danne Corporate Services, our mission is clear:
To empower organizations with the structure, compliance, and confidence needed to thrive in today’s regulated business environment.

We combine expertise, technology, and integrity to help businesses stay focused on what matters most, growth, sustainability, and good governance.
